Wild Elephant Project

Protecting Sri Lanka's majestic elephants and preserving their natural habitat. Join us in our mission to create harmony between wildlife and communities.

What We Do

Elephant Fence

Join a hands-on conservation effort to build and maintain safe, eco-friendly elephant fences.

Habitat Restoration

Restoring and expanding natural habitats through reforestation and sustainable land management practices.

Community Engagement

Working with local communities to develop sustainable livelihoods and reduce human-elephant conflict.

Education Programs

Teaching the next generation about wildlife conservation and the importance of protecting endangered species.

Research & Monitoring

Conducting cutting-edge research to better understand elephant behavior and improve conservation strategies.

Pre-School Teaching

Help young children (ages 3–5) learn through fun and creativity! Volunteers support local teachers with basic English, songs, art, storytelling, and play-based lessons that build confidence and curiosity.

Elephant Deterrent

A creative and compassionate wildlife project where volunteers prepare natural orange-based deterrents to help keep elephants safely away from villages and crops without harm.

Women Empowerment

Volunteers support local women to build confidence, learn new skills, and become financially independent. Activities include teaching English, computer skills, crafts, and health awareness helping women unlock their potential and strengthen their communities.

Our Impact

Measuring success through tangible results and lasting change

Conservation Success

Our efforts have led to significant improvements in elephant populations and habitat quality.

  • 25% increase in local elephant population
  • 500 hectares of habitat restored
  • Zero poaching incidents in protected areas
  • 80% reduction in human-elephant conflict

Community Benefits

Creating sustainable solutions that benefit both people and wildlife.

  • 200+ families supported with alternative livelihoods
  • 15 schools with conservation education programs
  • Local employment opportunities created
  • Eco-tourism revenue shared with communities

Global Recognition

Our innovative approaches are setting new standards in wildlife conservation.

  • International conservation awards
  • Partnership with leading research institutions
  • Model for elephant conservation worldwide
  • Featured in major conservation publications
